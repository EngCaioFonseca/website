8Knot is a Plotly Dash application that turns open source community data —
collected via Augur / CollectOSS — into interactive visualizations and
higher-order, ML-informed insights about community health, contributor activity
and project sustainability. It's part of Red Hat's Project Aspen, and I'm one of
its maintainers.
