Welcome. This is the first post on my rebuilt site.

For years my corner of the web was a static academic page that introduced me as
a PhD student. That chapter mattered — molecular communications, EEG, the
GLADIATOR project — but it stopped telling the whole story once I moved into
software engineering.

So I rebuilt the site from the ground up with a few goals:

- **One identity, two strengths.** Engineer *and* researcher, not one at the
  expense of the other.
- **Fast and accessible by default.** Static-generated, near-zero JavaScript,
  works on any device, respects reduced-motion and dark mode.
- **Easy to grow.** New posts, papers and projects are just files I drop in.

This blog is where I'll write about the things I build and the ideas I keep
circling back to — systems, machine learning, and the strange, fertile border
between biology and computation.

More soon.
